Meet the team behind OWEN

Our mission is to improve work-life using data and technology. OWEN is a first step in that endeavor. We have a slew of other products being developed in the people management space including a big data application for enhancing quality of hire, a machine learning based attrition predictor, and a platform for employee mobility. Our rockstar team consists of domain experts, data scientists, technology geeks and strategy consultants.
